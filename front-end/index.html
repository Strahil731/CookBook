    • Banitsa with cheese

      Product:

      • Fine Crusts-400gr
      • Yoghurt-400gr
      • Eggs-4
      • Cheese-300gr
      • Oil-100ml

      In a bowl, beat the eggs, add the yogurt and baking powder, mix. Crumble the cheese separately. Melt the butter in the oil to mix well. Brush the crusts with the fat two at a time. Pour evenly 5-6 tbsp on each pair of skins. of the mixture and sprinkle with cheese, so it is easier to control the amount. Roll the crusts of the roll on their short side to make the pieces thicker and the pie juicier. Roll the roll in a greased pan into a snail shape and spread a little grease on top.Place the tray in an oven preheated to 180Â°C on a medium rack and bake for 40-45 minutes until done.

    • Beans with sausage

      Product:

      • Beans-500gr
      • Macedonian sausage-3pieces
      • Jojen-2pinches dry
      • Parsley-3sprigs fresh
      • Carrots-1pc

      Washed beans are poured with cold water and boiled for 30 minutes on the stove. The water is discarded, about 1 liter and 400 ml of spring water are poured into a clay pot. Add carrot cut into half moons, finely chopped old onion, green pepper into strips, 2-3 tbsp. oil and beans. Cook for 1 hour in a cold oven with a lid. Heat the remaining oil in a pan and fry the sausage, cut into pieces, without reaching the end. Transfer the sausage to the pot. In the same oil, fry a chopped stalk of fresh onion, add red pepper, stir and pour the broth over the beans. Add salt to taste, fresh parsley and finely chopped chives.
